Chromostereoscopic Colour Shaded Relief

Description:

The purpose of this project is to explore the integration of terrestrial and hydrological DEM datasets with the goal of creating a map product that demonstrates the importance of depth using a chromostereoscopic colour shaded relief.

After the completion of the chromostereoscopic shaded relief, viewers of the map are able to see variations in depth with the aid of ChromaDepth glasses.

Project Requirements:
  • PCI Geomatica
  • EASI Scripting
  • Processing and merger of ETOPO1 and Globe elevation datasets
  • Colour shaded relief development
