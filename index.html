Projects

Spatial Analysis of Roman Britain - Cybercartographic Atlas:

During my graduate studies I performed spatial analysis on Roman military building practices in Britain. This research used a multi-method geomatics approach, including the use GIS with fuzzy set theory, remote sensing, and a cybercartography atlas.

Spatial Analysis of Arctic Fish Occurrence:

Using data provided by the Canadian Museum of Nature, species habitat modelling was performed, and thousands of species occurrence maps were generated through python scripts.

Hiking Trail Difficulty Analysis:

A fun research project which assessed the difficulty of multiple hiking trails based on terrain changes.
